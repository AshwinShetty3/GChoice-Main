Indian Cashew Nuts Exporters

India is one of the leading exporters of cashew kernels, offering high-quality, hand-processed whole cashews known for their creamy texture, crunchy bite, and rich flavor. Indian cashews are in demand across the Middle East, Europe, USA, Japan, and Southeast Asia.

At G-Choice Exports, we offer export-grade whole cashew kernels (W180, W210, W240, W320, W450) that are processed in FDA and APEDA-approved facilities, adhering to global food safety and hygiene standards. Our cashews are free from additives and preservatives and packed to maintain long shelf life and freshness.

Standard Packing Details

Grades Available W180 (King), W210, W240, W320 (popular), W450
Packaging Types Vacuum-sealed pouches in tins or cartons
Inner Packaging 2 x 25 lb (11.34 kg) vacuum bags = 22.68 kg net per carton
Outer Carton Size 49 x 33 x 20 cm (approx.)
Labeling Grade, net weight, batch number, packing date, origin, exporter details
Optional Packaging Retail packs: 250g, 500g, 1kg; Nitrogen-flushed pouches
Storage Conditions Cool, dry, pest-free environment (18–25°C recommended)
Palletization 700–720 cartons (16 MT approx.) per 20’ container
Shelf Life 6–9 months under proper storage
