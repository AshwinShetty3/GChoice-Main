Top Food Products Export Destinations from India

  • 31 Jul 2020

India is a country blessed with beautiful climatic and soil conditions. Thus, many agricultural products thrive in India. Indian products are exported to various countries in the world. Some of the top export destinations from India include the USA, Netherlands, the UK, UAE, Bangladesh so on and so forth.

Major Food Export Destinations

India exports food products to different corners of the world. Indian cuisine is popular across the world and therefore the demand for Indian food products has seen an exponential rise in the past few years. The major export destinations for Indian food include the US, UK, UAE, Germany, Thailand, and many more.

Floriculture

Floriculture basically means the cultivation of flowers. India has favorable climatic and soil conditions for the growth of most floriculture products. Here are the main countries involved in the floriculture export from India:

  • USA

    India exports flowers to many countries in the world. The United States of America (USA) imports a large number of floriculture products from India. In 2019-20, India exported 3276.08 Metric tons of floriculture products worth $19486.73 to the United States.

  • Netherlands

    The Netherlands is another country which imports several floriculture products from India. In 2019-2020, India exported 1377.08 Metric tons of floriculture products to the Netherlands. The approximate value of the export is around $10966.53.

  • UK

    India exports a large number of floriculture products to the United Kingdom (UK) every year. In 2019-2020, India exported 1236.74 Metric tons of floriculture products to the UK. This export in 2019-20 of floriculture products to the UK was worth around $5742.88.

  • Germany

    India exports huge quantities of floriculture products to Germany every year. In the year 2019-2020, India exported 1112.52 Metric tons of floriculture products to Germany. This export in 2019-20 of floriculture products to Germany was worth around $5749.46.

  • UAE

    India also exports floriculture products to the United Arab Emirates(UAE). In the year 2019-2020, India exported 1499.07 Metric tons of floriculture products to UAE. This export in 2019-20 of floriculture products to UAE was worth around $4621.48.

Fruits & Vegetables Seeds

Fruits and vegetable seeds export from India has also been increasing over the years. Many countries like the Netherlands, USA, Pakistan, and so on import fruit and vegetable seeds from India. Here are the main countries which import fruits and vegetable seeds from India:

  • USA

    India exports many fruits and vegetable seeds to the United States of America. In the year 2019-2020, India exported 671.79 Metric tons of Fruits and Vegetables Seeds to the US. This export was worth around $16.78 million.

  • Pakistan

    India exports numerous fruits and vegetable seeds to Pakistan. In the year 2019-2020, India exported 1,517.22 Metric tons of Fruits and Vegetable seeds to Pakistan. This export was worth around $4.43 million.

  • Bangladesh

    India exports many fruits and vegetable seeds to Bangladesh. In the year 2019-2020, India exported 6,951.18 Metric tons of Fruits and Vegetable seeds to Pakistan. This export was worth around $10.85 million.

  • Thailand

    India exports various fruits and vegetable seeds to Thailand. In the year 2019-2020, India exported 157.93 Metric tons of Fruits and Vegetable seeds to Thailand. This export was worth around $6.75million.
