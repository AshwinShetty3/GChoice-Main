Now, let’s come to minute details. ‘Apple’ contains a hormone, named acetylcholine, and few scientific facts state that this hormone is responsible for boosting up the human brain, especially it improves one’s memories. You will be surprised to know one more fact that people nowadays are dying more from heart attacks and strokes. And if anyone knows eating apples reduces one’s cholesterol level by two-third and trust me it’s an incredible fact. Are Apples weight-loss-friendly or fattening?

No matter how much nutrition an apple holds, but we still wonder whether they are ‘fattening’ or ‘weight-loss’ friendly. To your surprise, a medium-sized apple contains 86% of water, thus it helps in hydrating one’s body as well as calorie-density. Apart from this, if we try to analyze other scientific facts, it contains loss-friendly fibre, and many studies show that intake of high amount fibre results in weight-loss. Thus, I would just like to tell those of you who believe eating fruits may increase your weight, it’s just a myth, and nothing of that is sought. So just try to remember one thing “A healthy outside, starts from healthy inside”.

Now let us try to look at the other dimensions of it. We, students, have a general tendency of not waking up early in the morning, so what do you do to wake up early? To be frank, I prefer to have a cup of coffee or tea, because I believe they are the best medicines that one should go for. But surprisingly, as I was searching many facts for writing this article, I came across one interesting fact, which stated, not coffee, but eating an apple is much more efficient in waking you up than a coffee. Even though I was not able to digest this fact, I tried to probe deeper and deeper, and then I got some information about it.

Are apples better at waking you up?

As stated above, apart from providing nutrition and other health benefits, it proves quite beneficial in waking us up early in the morning. What can sound more interesting than this? It is difficult, especially for the younger generation who are the victims of either insomnia or have excess sleep syndrome symptoms. Though it does not contain any kind of caffeine, it still proves to be better than coffee. You all must be wondering what’s the reason behind this? It’s just because it contains 12-13 grams of natural sugar, thus helping in improving our sleep cycle.

The things which we ignore the most prove to be the best things for us. No matter how much we avoid eating fruits, but sooner or later we will regret not knowing the true benefits of it.
